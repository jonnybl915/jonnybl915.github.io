H2Know

This app allows users to know when to water their plants. The user picks from our preexisting list of houseplants, and after watering the plant, clicks a button which starts the watering timer. When the timer is done the user knows to water their plant again.

H2Know. So you don't have to remember.

This was an extra-curricular weekend assignment which I worked on in tandem with one front ender. I used Java with Spring and Hibernate, and H2.
